About Us

We invite you to our euphoric carnival, bustling with cackles and thrills. Buy a ticket to some of the most exhilarating rides of India. However, beware of feeling your heart in your mouth and being scared out of your wits as the wind hits you in the face to leave your body shaking like a leaf. Following the adrenaline rush, you'll wander around to taste Delhi's scrumptious street food, followed by a dazzling promenade to thrifting some of the finest collection of handmade artifacts. Along with taking home wonderful memories of the evening, you will also get a chance to win prizes by playing some exciting games. To end your night on a dramatic climax, you'll witness a show of glittering jets of firecrackers.
